Descreption

This Persian fried fish recipe is popular during Nowruz (Persian New Year) since fish is a symbol of life. Serve with Sabzi Polo.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground saffron
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice (from 1 lemon)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a very large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Transfer fish to a wire rack. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.
